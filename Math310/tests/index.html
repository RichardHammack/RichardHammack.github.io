Note for Fall 2018: There are some notable ways that the 2007 tests differ from this semester's midterm. First, the above tests are take-home. Our midterm will be in-class. Second, the above tests are designed for a MWF class lasting 50 minutes, with a total of three tests, whereas we will have only one test (the midterm).

However, the above do offer good opportunities for midterm practice. Bear in mind, however, that some of the above take-home questions may be slightly longer than typical in-class questions. (So don't panic!)

  • Test #1 (above): All problems, except 5(a), but including 5(b).
  • Note that LU-factorization will not appear on our midterm.
