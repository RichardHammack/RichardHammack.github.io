Calculus Help


VCU's Mathematics Department has a walk-in help center. See the schedule at https://math.vcu.edu/current-students/help-center/


THE Campus Learning Center (CLC) offers free tutoring for students in MATH 201. You can schedule individual appointments, or attend drop-in sessions, as described below.
Individual Appointments: The CLC offers hour-long individual appointments for students looking for scheduled personal support. You can schedule appointments via Navigate. (Refer to this video walkthroughdemonstrating how to make appointments.) You can have two weekly appointments and must make appointments at least 48 hours in advance.

Drop-In Tutoring: Drop-In tutoring is a more informal tutoring session that students can attend without an appointment. Check the Tutoring Schedule. Tutors often work with multiple students and promote group work. Students can attend Drop-In as often as they'd like during the week, with no limit.
