Calculus Help Sessions


VCU's Mathematics Department has a walk-in help center. See the schedule at https://math.vcu.edu/current-students/help-center/


THE Campus Learning Center (CLC) offers free tutoring for students in MATH 200. You can schedule individual appointments, or attend drop-in sessions, as described below.
Individual Appointments: The CLC offers hour-long online individual appointments for students looking for scheduled personal support. You can schedule appointments via Navigate. (Refer to this video walkthrough demonstrating how to make appointments.) You can have two weekly appointments and must make appointments at least 48 hours in advance.

Drop-In Tutoring: Drop-In tutoring is a more informal tutoring session that students can attend without an appointment. After checking our Drop-In Tutoring schedule online, students can come to the Drop-In Room in Hibbs 107 to work with a tutor. Tutors often work with multiple students and promote group work. Students can attend Drop-In as often as they'd like during the week, with no limit.
